We don’t just write and share our dreams - we realize them

Writing down goals are probably very mainstream and something that you might heard of it from time to time. But what about sharing them? You probably might not have heard about it at all, at least the benefits of it. Well, you have come to the right page! Our website is a platform where one can write their goals and share them with others, anonymously or non-anonymously, whatever your choice is.

Here are some benefits of sharing your dreams:

  1. They can hold you accountable.
  2. You can gain more clarity.
  3. You’ve got someone to help you.
  4. You’ll feel more motivated.
  5. And MOST IMPORTANTLY, it improves your chances of reaching them.

Whew! Above are the benefits for sharing your goals with others, and for more information, look out for this website: Career 101: 5 Reasons Why You Need To Share Your Goals With Others
